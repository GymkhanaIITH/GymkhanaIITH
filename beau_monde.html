Vibes

Welcome to VIBES - The music club of IITH! We conduct regular jamming sessions, guitar sessions, vocal sessions etc. to encourage learning new things and having fun. We want to bring out the musician in everyone. Join us if you love to sing, play or write music! Vibes was the winner of the "Best Cultural Club" title in the year 2017-18!
